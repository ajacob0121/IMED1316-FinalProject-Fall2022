Open House Schedule

Come by and see us!! Succulents, with their striking forms, unusual colors, and easy-care reputation, are fantastic design elements in outdoor and indoor gardens and landscaping. Whether planted in mass, as ground cover or garden accents or featured in containers and terrariums, succulents have much to offer landscapers and home gardeners. Their versatility and striking appearance make succulents perfect for unique projects, containers, and gardens.
